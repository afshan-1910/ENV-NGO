About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Zona Green began as a small group of environmental enthusiasts who wanted to make a difference. Over the years, we have grown into a global movement, working tirelessly to protect and restore our planet.

Our Vision

We envision a world where humans live in harmony with nature, where every individual takes responsibility for preserving the environment for future generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable practices, raise awareness about environmental issues, and empower communities to take action for a greener future.

Our Achievements

  • Planted over 1 million trees worldwide.
  • Conducted 500+ environmental awareness campaigns.
  • Reduced plastic waste by 30% in partnered communities.
  • Collaborated with 100+ organizations globally.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate environmentalists, scientists, and volunteers who are dedicated to making a positive impact. Together, we work towards a common goal: a sustainable and green planet.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to promote environmental sustainability and create a greener future.

Tree Plantation

We organize large-scale tree plantation drives to restore forests and combat climate change. Join us to plant trees and make a difference!

Awareness Campaigns

We conduct workshops, seminars, and social media campaigns to educate communities about environmental issues and sustainable practices.

Waste Management

We implement waste segregation, recycling, and composting programs to reduce landfill waste and promote a circular economy.

Renewable Energy

We promote the use of solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources to reduce carbon emissions and dependency on fossil fuels.

Water Conservation

We work on projects to conserve water resources, including rainwater harvesting and efficient irrigation systems.

Wildlife Protection

We support initiatives to protect endangered species and their habitats, ensuring biodiversity for future generations.
